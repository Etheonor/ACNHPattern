# About Sweetter!

## What is Sweetter ?

Sweeter is a cool website that allow you to read many content of Twitter, but without the bitter!

## Why did you build this website?

Because I was tired of beeing mad every time I opened Twitter. I know there is a lot of nice content on this platform, but every time I visit Twitter, I'm mad about something!

The idea is to check verified content, about pleasant topics, and not worry about what you can find in the middle of your tweeter feed.
